Introduction to Great Danes

The Great Dane is a gentle giant who is known for his loyalty, intelligence, and trainability. This breed is the tallest dog breed in the world, and they can weigh up to 175 pounds. Great Danes are known for being gentle and patient with children, and they are also very intelligent and easy to train. They are a great choice for families with children or active adults. Great Danes are originally from Germany, and they were bred to hunt wild boars. They are still known for their athleticism and strength, but they are also very gentle and loving dogs. They make great companions for people of all ages, and they are sure to bring joy to any family. Some people choose to dock the ears and tails of their Great Dane, but this has no health benefits for the dog and is purely cosmetic. You can see the fawn color below with their ears docked.
